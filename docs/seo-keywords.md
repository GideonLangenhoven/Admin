## How to use this doc

For each keyword below, do one of three things:
- ✅ **Approve** — I'll build a page or feature for it
- ⚠️ **Swap** — propose a replacement keyword in the "swap for" column
- ❌ **Reject** — tell me why, I'll learn and remove

Columns explained:
- **Intent**: `B` = brand / navigational, `C` = commercial (buyer evaluating), `T` = transactional (ready to sign up), `I` = informational (researching problem)
- **Conf**: my confidence the keyword matches your ICP and the SA market (1=low, 5=high)
- **Priority**: the sequence to build the target page. P1 = first 10 pages, P2 = next 15, P3 = later.
- **Target page**: the URL we'd build
