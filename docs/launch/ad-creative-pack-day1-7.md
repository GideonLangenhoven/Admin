# CapeKayak Ad Creative Pack (Days 1-7)

## Goal
Drive qualified operator demos to `/operators` with transparent pricing and clear plan fit.

## Creative Guardrails
- Always show commercial terms clearly:
  - Setup fee: `R3,500` once-off
  - Starter: `R1,500/month`
  - Growth: `R3,000/month`
  - Pro: `R6,500/month`
- Do not hide add-ons:
  - Landing page build: `R3,500` first page
  - Additional pages: `R1,500` each
  - Hosting: `R500/month` when add-on is active
- Keep CTA singular per ad:
  - `Book a demo`

## UTM Convention
- `utm_source`: `meta`, `google`, `linkedin`
- `utm_medium`: `paid_social`, `paid_search`, `paid_social_b2b`
- `utm_campaign`: `launch_v3_d{day}_{angle}`
- `utm_content`: creative identifier (example: `meta_static_a1_v1`)
- `utm_term`: keyword or audience cluster

## Weekly Optimization Rules
- Keep at least 2 active creatives per channel at all times.
- Pause any creative below baseline CTR after 1,500+ impressions.
- Shift budget to ads with best qualified-demo cost, not lowest click cost.
- Refresh the worst-performing creative every 3-4 days in launch week.
