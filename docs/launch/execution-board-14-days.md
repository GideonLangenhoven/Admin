# CapeKayak Launch Execution Board (14 Days)

## Roles
- `FOUNDER`: final approvals, sales calls, partnerships
- `PMM`: messaging, content, channel ops
- `SALES`: outreach, demos, follow-up
- `PRODUCT`: product readiness, instrumentation, QA

## Success Criteria (Day 14)
- Public offer live and consistent across web, sales deck, and billing.
- At least 10 qualified demos completed.
- First closed-won customers on Starter/Growth.
- Top-up and landing-page add-on billing paths validated with real payments.
- Daily KPI tracking running from `docs/launch/metrics-sql.md`.

## Daily Operating Rhythm
- 08:30: KPI standup (15 minutes): traffic, demos, paid conversions, cap hits, top-ups.
- 13:00: Midday check: inbound response SLA and demo attendance.
- 17:00: End-of-day summary: blockers, wins, and day+1 priorities.

## Required Launch Assets
- `/operators` page (primary CTA path).
- `/case-study/cape-kayak` page (proof path).
- `/compare/manual-vs-disconnected-tools` page (objection path).
- Sales deck aligned with locked offer.
- Demo script aligned with plan thresholds and add-ons.
- Follow-up email templates for no-show, trial, and proposal.

## Go/No-Go Rules
- Go only if cap enforcement, top-up crediting, and hosting billing are all verified in production.
- Go only if at least one owner is assigned to same-day inbound response.
- Delay full launch if billing or booking status logic is inconsistent.
